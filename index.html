Hi!

I am a fourth-year PhD student at UAB-BSE, Barcelona. I want to better understand how people and the environment they are a part of interact with each other. Specifically, I use network theory to model the social and economic context and investigate how and to what extent it affects individual beliefs and actions, and market outcomes. (So far) My research questions are grounded on finance and information.
